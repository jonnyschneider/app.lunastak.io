# Humble Ventures Decision Stack

## Vision (3+ years)
*To be the trusted strategic partner that empowers growth-stage SaaS leadership teams to turn strategic ambiguity into decisive action and measurable outcomes.*

## Strategy (12-18 months)
*Solve tomorrow's problems for product leaders with systematic expert evaluation, build partnership delivery model that preserves personal agency and wellness, and create direct client relationships that provide stability and selective high-impact opportunities.*

**Three Strategic Pillars:**
1. **Future-Focused Problem Solving** - Next-order challenges, differentiated from commodity product consulting
2. **Agency-Preserving Scaling** - Partnership model that maintains control and energy 
3. **Direct Client Relationships** - Own paper, work toward 'anchors', hunt for selective high-impact

## Objectives (12-18 months)

### Future-Focused Problem Solving (12 months)
- 100% of engagements address next-order strategic challenges (not operational firefighting)
- Launch "Sniff Test" with 15+ assessments demonstrating unique strategic insight
- 30% of conversations generated through proactive positioning vs. reactive referrals

### Agency-Preserving Scaling (18 months)  
- 100% of time spent where you want to spend it (energy creation vs. energy taking)
- 66% of consulting engagements on your paper (vs. sub-contracting)
- 8-10 high-trust partner network for collaborative delivery

### Direct Client Relationships (18 months)
- 33% of income from steady anchor source (bills-paying foundation)
- 70/30 revenue split: predictable base + selective high-impact projects
- 100% income from professional services invoices (zero employment contracts)

## Opportunities/Initiatives (3-12 months)

### Future-Focused Problem Solving
- **Launch "Sniff Test" offering** (3 months) → Package, price <$1k, run 3-5 pilots
- **Content positioning shift** (6 months) → Growth-stage SaaS leadership focus, next-order challenges
- **Proactive engagement** (9 months) → Systematic outreach to ICP audience, speaking, workshop delivery, partnerships

### Agency-Preserving Scaling  
- **Partner network mapping** (6 months) → Identify 8-10 established freelancers via Airtree/agencies
- **Boundary system implementation** (3 months) → Time tracking, alignment scoring, energy audit
### Direct Client Relationships
- **Anchor client assessment** (6 months) → Identify current relationships for retainer conversion
- **Service flow systematization** (9 months) → Document Sniff Test → Strategy → Experimentation progression  
- **Premium positioning reinforcement** (12 months) → Maintain rates while scaling entry points

## Principles

1. **Strategic clients** even over any paying client 
2. **Energy-giving partnerships** even over higher-margin solo work 
3. **Own paper** even over guaranteed work
4. **Boundary adherence** even over client accommodation
